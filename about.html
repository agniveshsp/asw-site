About Us

"Authentic Ayurveda from God's Own Country, Kerala"

Established in 2020, Aswamedha Healthcare is a dedicated Ayurvedic hospital and herbal manufacturing company committed to delivering high-quality, safe, and effective natural healthcare solutions. Rooted in the authentic Ayurvedic traditions of Kerala, God's Own Country, the company promotes holistic wellness through herbal formulations that address the growing demand for natural, preventive, and sustainable healthcare.

Built on the principles of purity, integrity, and responsibility, Aswamedha combines traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with modern manufacturing practices, scientific research, and stringent quality standards. The company offers a diverse portfolio of products, including classical Ayurvedic medicines, herbal supplements, wellness formulations, medicated oils, personal care products, and nutraceuticals. Every product is crafted using carefully selected natural ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers and processed to ensure purity, safety, efficacy, and consistency.

Aswamedha's foundation is strengthened by a proud lineage of Ayurvedic doctors and Vaidyas. With roots in a family-run Ayurvedic Treatment Centre in Kerala, the founder brings generations of expertise and a deep commitment to authentic Ayurvedic practices. Each product undergoes rigorous quality evaluation under the direct supervision of the founder and his family, ensuring the highest standards of excellence.

Over the years, the company has built a strong presence in the healthcare and wellness sector through collaborations with healthcare practitioners, pharmacies, distributors, wellness centres, and online sales platforms. Its focus on innovation, regulatory compliance, sustainability, ethical business practices, and customer satisfaction has earned the trust of a growing customer base.

Looking ahead, Aswamedha Healthcare aims to strengthen its manufacturing capabilities through strategic collaboration with a Japanese herbal manufacturing company, expand its global market presence, explore export opportunities, and establish itself as a recognised leader in the Ayurvedic and herbal healthcare industry.
